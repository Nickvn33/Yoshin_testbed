Beginner

Making the decision to start Karate is a very BIG step in your life. Congratulations. We endeavor to present to you the true way of Karate, or “Karate-Do”, that is, a way to develop total fitness: mind, body and spirit. Making an investment in your personal development isn't to be taken lightly, and as such, we commit to giving you the best instruction possible.

Our system allows you to ease into the training program slowly and safely. It is not necessary that you have any previous karate experience. You progress at a pace consistent with your ability and level of fitness. Everyone is treated as an individual. We fully appreciate that you may not be super-fit or flexible right from the start. We will work with you and track your progress as a beginner to ensure you realize your true potential as a student of Karate.

For new beginners we offer the first month free.

Please wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing such as a t-shirt and full-length tracksuit pants and arrive 15 minutes early so we can show you around before the class starts.

In these lessons you’ll learn some basic karate techniques, and get a feel for how things operate in a karate class, so you can move comfortably into regular training.

You will not participate in any sparring or fighting until you have earned your 10th Kyu (Yellow belt). This safety measure is in place to protect both you and other students. Learning proper distance and control is a key part of the curriculum.

Previous Martial Arts Training

Have you studied martial arts before? Would you like to try again? At our school we happily accept former martial arts students who wish to get started again. We fully appreciate your previous experience when you re-commence training. Although you may have achieved a particular belt/rank in another style, you will be assessed for recognition of prior learning. The levels you achieved will be considered and we will gladly expedite your progress with us as you learn our syllabus. We encourage you to see our dojo and watch a class to see if our style is right for you.
