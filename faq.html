FAQ

Can I keep my rank from another dojo?

Your rank will be evaluated based on your skill level, and a decision will be made accordingly

Can I wear my Gi from my previous dojo?

Yes, you can wear your Gi. We also offer club badges that you can sew onto it to represent our dojo

What if I miss grading nights due to illness or other circumstances?

We hold 3 to 4 grading nights per year. Depending on your situation, we may offer special accommodations

Can I train at another dojo as well?

Absolutely! We encourage training at other dojos. Many of our advanced students at Yoshin have trained in various styles or with other dojos
